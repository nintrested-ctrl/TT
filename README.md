TimeTrack
=========

TimeTrack is a lightweight Windows screen-time tracker.

It helps you see:
- total time spent on your PC
- app/software usage
- idle time
- categories
- daily, weekly, and monthly reports

Activation:
1. Open TimeTrack.
2. Enter the exact email address used at Gumroad checkout.
3. Click Copy / Request Key.
4. The license request is copied to your clipboard, and Gmail may open in your browser.
5. Paste the copied request into an email gieven
6. Enter the license key sent back to you.

Your license key is tied to your checkout email and Device ID.
The app may show your buyer email, license ID, and Device ID inside the app and CSV exports as an anti-resale watermark.

Support email:
Will be provided in installer

Privacy:
TimeTrack stores tracking data locally on your Windows PC.
The app does not secretly send your Device ID. You choose to send the request email.
